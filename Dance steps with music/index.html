Hello Everyone! I Am going to teach you contemporary dance steps

Step:1

First of all hands up and both hands fingers should be close to each other and freely moving.Your one leg should be forward and other backward.

Step:2

Slightly bend your waist towards right and the right hand should be slanting towards your right leg.Your left hand should be up flowing in the air towards the sky and holding your left leg.

Step:3

Take your left leg backward and bend your waist towards back.Your left hand should be moving freely in upward direction and right hand should be in downward direction but in 45 degrees from legs.

Step:4

Move your upper potion of the body towards front and your right hand straight up in 45 degrees towards sky.Then slightly bend your that hand from elbow in backward direction.Your left leg should be upward and your left hand should be holding your left leg.

Step:5

Take your right hand upward and your left leg should be upward going from side of your body.Then hold that leg upward with both the hands.
